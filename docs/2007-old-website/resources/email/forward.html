How To...

Set up your MITS account with a mail program e.g. Outlook Express

  1. Log in at http://mail.mits.org.au

  2. Once you can see your inbox, click on "Settings"

  3. Select "Forwarding and POP"

  4. Enter in the email address you want to forward everything to and click "Save Changes"


    If you use Hotmail...

  5. Send email from your MITS email address via hotmail - instrukshuns
