Your MITS Email Account

There are several ways you can use your email account:

  1. Log in at http://mail.mits.org.au/ and use the Gmail interface (send and receive)
  2. Set up your account in a mail program e.g. Outlook Express (send and receive) - instrukshuns
  3. Forward all emails to another account (receive only) - instrukshuns
  4. Send from your MITS email address via Hotmail (send only) - instrukshuns

Let's all use our MITS emails for sending external correspondence, cos it makes us look impressive like.

Hints:

I recommend Option 1. I personally find it much better to keep MITS related emails separate to my personal emails. The Gmail system is very different to Hotmail etc, but it's quite good once you get the hang of it.

Option 2 would be good if you already use Outlook Express or a similar mail program.

Otherwise, options 3 and 4 make a good combination.
